## Adopted for Cristo Rei V1

- Immersive hero + inner page hero template
- Dark header, centered nav, social icons
- News cards (carousel on home, grid on `/noticias`)
- Schedule list layout
- Editorial white content areas with generous spacing
- Low animation (fade-up; optional card hover)

## Not adopted in V1

- Gold/black palette (awaiting Cristo Rei colors)
- Donation band, virtual candle, vídeos, downloads, sacrament pages
- Floating side button
- 8+ top-level nav items (we use 5)
- História da devoção multi-page structure (communities replace this as differentiator)

## Cristo Rei differentiator

**24 communities hub** — not present in reference; central to our `/comunidades` and home preview section.
